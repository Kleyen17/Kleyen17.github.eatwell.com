Diet Plans: Pros & Cons

Compare different diet plans based on their benefits and potential drawbacks.

Diet Plan Why Follow It? Why Not?
Keto Diet May support weight loss, improved brain function, and stable blood sugar levels. Can be restrictive, may cause nutrient deficiencies, and has potential long-term health risks.
Vegan Diet Ethical choice, promotes heart health, and can reduce risk of chronic diseases. Requires careful nutrient planning to avoid deficiencies (B12, iron, omega-3s).
Mediterranean Diet Rich in healthy fats, supports heart health, and is backed by strong research. Can be expensive due to fresh ingredients; portion control is important.
Paleo Diet Encourages whole foods, reduces processed food intake, and may improve gut health. Eliminates grains and dairy, which can lead to nutrient gaps; restrictive for some.
Intermittent Fasting Supports weight management, may enhance metabolic function, and simplifies meal planning. Not ideal for people with certain medical conditions; can be challenging to maintain.
Gluten-Free Diet Essential for those with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity; may reduce inflammation. Unnecessary for those without sensitivity; gluten-free alternatives can be highly processed.
